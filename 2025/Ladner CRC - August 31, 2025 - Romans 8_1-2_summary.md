• The concept of condemnation being eliminated for Christians
• Jesus' life, death, and resurrection brought a future verdict into the present reality
• Union with Christ makes one declared not guilty and free from fear of judgment
• God's address is now in Christ, making His people secure as Christ himself
• Condemnation is only for those who are not in Christ
• The Christian life is defined by being enclosed and encircled safely in Christ
• The law of the spirit gives life and sets us free from the law of sin and death
• There are two laws: Moses' law (right but not might) and the law of sin (might but no right)
• The law of the spirit has both right and might, giving us the ability to keep God's law
• Before receiving the Spirit, we were like a rock, unable to do anything; now we can live by the law of freedom and "fly"
• The Holy Spirit transforms people's hearts, changing them from the inside out (example given of David's transformation)
• Romans 8 doesn't have commands for us to obey, but rather calls us to believe what God says: there is no condemnation for those in Christ Jesus.
